# UniKidz – Inclusive Education for Kids (Website Design Reference)

## Overview
This image is a website showcase/mockup for **UniKidz**, a children's education and reading platform.

## Visual Style
- Playful and child-friendly design
- Large rounded/scalloped section dividers
- Bright primary colors:
  - Deep Blue
  - Red
  - Yellow
  - Green
  - White
- Cartoon-inspired geometric decorations
- Large photography featuring children reading and learning

### 1. Home Page
- Hero banner with children reading
- Featured books section

- Product/book cards
- Newsletter signup section
- Footer

### 2. Shop Page

- Shop by category
- Product carousel/cards

- Footer

### 3. About Us Page
- Hero image

### 4. FAQ / Contact Page
- FAQ accordion layout
- Contact form
- Footer

## Design Elements
- Rounded wave/scallop separators
- Floating decorative shapes
- Large typography
- Color-block sections
- Card-based content organization
- Friendly illustrations and imagery

## Inspiration Notes
This design style is suitable for:
- Schools
- Kindergartens
- Children's libraries
- Educational startups
- Reading platforms
- Kids activity websites

## Key Takeaway
The design combines educational trust with playful visuals, using bold colors, rounded shapes, and engaging imagery to appeal to both children and parents.
