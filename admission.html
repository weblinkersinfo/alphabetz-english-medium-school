Admission Process

Simple Steps to Enroll

Our admission process is designed to be straightforward and stress-free. Follow these easy steps to secure your child's spot.

1

📩 Submit an Enquiry

Fill out the online enquiry form on our website or visit the school office. Our team will get back to you within 24 hours with all the details you need.

2

🏫 Visit the Campus

Schedule a campus tour to experience our vibrant learning environment first-hand. Meet our teachers, explore the classrooms, and see our facilities in action.

3

📄 Collect the Application Form

Obtain the official admission form from the school office or download it from our website. The form can also be picked up during your campus visit.

4

📋 Submit Documents

Submit the completed application form along with the required documents (listed below). Ensure all photocopies are attested and originals are brought for verification.

5

🤝 Interaction Round

A friendly, informal interaction with the child and parents. This is not a test — it's a conversation to understand your child's interests, learning style, and comfort level.

6

✅ Confirmation & Fee Payment

Once selected, you'll receive a confirmation letter. Complete the fee payment to finalize enrollment. Welcome to the Alphabet'z School family!

Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions? We've Got Answers

Here are the most commonly asked questions about our admission process. If you need more help, don't hesitate to contact us.

What is the minimum age for Play Group admission?

+

Children must be at least 2.5 years old as of June 1st of the admission year. Age verification is done through the birth certificate submitted during the application process.

Is there an entrance exam for admission?

+

No, there is no formal entrance exam. We conduct a friendly, informal interaction with the child and parents. The aim is to understand the child's interests and comfort level — not to test them.

Can I pay the fees in installments?

+

Yes! The annual tuition fee can be paid in 2 convenient installments — one at the beginning of each term.

Does the school provide transport facilities?

+

Yes, we offer GPS-tracked school bus services covering all major areas of Kolhapur. Every bus has a trained attendant to ensure your child's safety. Transport fees are separate from tuition fees and vary based on distance.

What is the student-to-teacher ratio?

+

We maintain a low student-to-teacher ratio of approximately 20:1 to ensure every child receives personalized attention. For Play Group classes, the ratio is even lower at 15:1.

Is mid-session admission possible?

+

Mid-session admissions are considered on a case-by-case basis, subject to seat availability. A transfer certificate from the previous school and the latest report card are mandatory for mid-session entries.
