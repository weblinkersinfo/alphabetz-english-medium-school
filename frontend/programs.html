Programs Designed to Inspire

From the first playful steps to building academic confidence, our carefully designed programs ensure every child grows at their own pace — with joy and curiosity.

🎪

Play Group

2.5yrs+
Pre-Primary

Where Learning Begins with Play

The Play Group program is designed to introduce your child to the world of school through fun, play, and sensory exploration. Every activity is crafted to develop motor skills, social awareness, and a love for learning.

🎵Rhymes, Songs & Storytelling
🎨Art, Craft & Sensory Play
🔤Alphabet & Number Recognition
🤝Social Skills & Group Activities
🏃Free Play & Outdoor Time
🧸

Nursery

A nurturing environment focused on early socialization, sensory play, and basic conceptual learning to foster a love for education.

🎶Rhymes & Finger Play

🧸Sensory & Motor Skills
🧩Basic Shapes & Colors

Introduction to structured learning, focusing on vocabulary, motor skills, and interactive activities that encourage early independence.

1st Standard

6yrs+
Foundation Year

Building the Strong Foundation

The transition from play-based learning to structured education. We introduce phonics, basic numeracy, and early writing while keeping the environment warm and encouraging.

📖Phonics & Early Reading
🔢Basic Mathematics & Counting
✍️Handwriting & Creative Expression
🌍Environmental Awareness
🗣️Spoken English & Communication
🚀

2nd Standard

7yrs+
Growth Year

Expanding Horizons

Students develop reading fluency, creative writing, and mathematical thinking. Science curiosity is ignited through hands-on experiments and nature study.

📚Reading Fluency & Comprehension
✏️Creative Writing & Paragraphs
➕Addition, Subtraction & Shapes
🔬Basic Science & Experiments
🌿Environmental Studies (EVS)
🔬

3rd Standard

8yrs+
Exploration Year

Deeper Knowledge & Critical Thinking

Project-based learning takes center stage. Students develop analytical skills, collaborative problem-solving, and confidence in presenting their ideas.

📊Multiplication, Division & Fractions
📝Essay Writing & Grammar
🧪Advanced Science & Projects
🗺️Social Studies & Geography
💻Introduction to Computers
🏆

4th Standard

9yrs+
Mastery Year

The Launchpad for Higher Education

Our most advanced program prepares confident learners with strong academics, leadership opportunities, competitive exam readiness, and public speaking skills.

📐Advanced Mathematics & Problem Solving
📖Literature, Comprehension & Debate
🔬Science Projects & Innovation
🏅Competitive Exam Preparation
🎤Leadership & Public Speaking
Beyond Academics

Extra-Curricular Activities

We believe in the all-round development of every child. These activities run alongside academics throughout the year.

🏃

Sports & Games

Cricket, football, running, yoga, and annual sports day competitions.

🎵

Music & Dance

Classical and western music classes, dance workshops, and stage performances.

🎨

Art & Craft

Drawing, painting, clay modeling, origami, and craft projects for creativity.

🎭

Drama & Theatre

Skits, role-playing, puppet shows, and annual drama festival participation.

📖

Reading Club

Weekly storytelling sessions, book reviews, and reading competitions.

🌱

Nature & Eco Club

Gardening, nature walks, recycling projects, and environment awareness.
